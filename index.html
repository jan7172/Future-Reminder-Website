Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Location Triggers

Set any address or point of interest as the trigger for your reminder. Arrive — get notified. Simple.

Automatic Notifications

No tapping, no checking. Future Reminder fires your notification the moment you step into range.

100% Private
